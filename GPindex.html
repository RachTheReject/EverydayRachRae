KoKo Head Hike

1,048. That's how many stairs it takes to get to the top of this mountain. Making it to the top of this mountain was a momumental moment for me because of my sister. And before you ask, yes that is her looking back at me telling me to hurry up before it gets dark out! My sister loves to hike.

This was my first time and she didn't take me up the stairs, she led me up the side of the mountain. I remeber asking her multiple times to call the helicopter for rescue because I wasn't going to make it. Of course she said no everytime, but that was all I needed. Even though I kept asking for rescue, I knew I was going to rescue myself by continuing to put one foot in front of the other.
